Instant Answer Center

You can cancel an order within 1 hour of placing it directly from your Account Dashboard under "My Orders". After 1 hour, the vendor may have packaged the item, and you will need to initiate a return request instead.

Stackly is a marketplace connecting thousands of independent vendors. Each vendor ships from their own regional warehouse. You can check the estimated delivery times and dispatch locations on each specific product details page.

All verified vendors on Stackly are bound by our platform-wide 30-day return policy. Items must be in their original packaging, unused, and showing no signs of wear. Return shipping labels are printable from your account center.
