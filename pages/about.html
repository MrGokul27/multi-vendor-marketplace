The Stackly Guarantee

Uncompromising Standards: The Stackly Lab Guarantee

We don't just list products; we certify them. Every vendor's catalog goes through random batch testing in our in-house Quality Assurance Lab.

Power tools are checked for voltage stability and load tolerance, hand tools are tested for torque and tensile strength, and home appliances undergo rigorous thermal and safety audits. If a tool doesn't meet international ISO safety certifications, it doesn't get shipped. That's our commitment.

Vendor Support

Supporting Local Workshops & Global Manufacturers

Stackly was founded on the belief that small workshops and family-owned hardware businesses deserve a global stage. Our Vendor Empowerment Hub provides sellers with AI-driven inventory analytics, predictive demand forecasting, and smart shipping integrations.

As a heavy-goods marketplace, we take environmental stewardship seriously. We are proud to run the Green Hardware Initiative, which includes carbon-neutral shipping on heavy machinery purchases and battery-recycling partnerships for cordless power tools.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Every seller on Stackly undergoes a rigorous background check, including verification of business licenses, tax documents, warehouse audits, and product quality checks. We do this to maintain high standards and secure buyer trust.

Stackly uses an advanced escrow-style payment framework. When you make a purchase, the money is secured until the vendor confirms shipment and tracking data is validated. We support major cards, PayPal, and multi-currency payouts.

Since Stackly is a multi-vendor platform, items in your cart might belong to different vendors. If so, they will ship separately directly from each vendor's warehouse. You will receive unique tracking numbers for each shipment.
