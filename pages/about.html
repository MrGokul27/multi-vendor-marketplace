FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Every seller on Stackly undergoes a rigorous background check, including verification of business licenses, tax documents, warehouse audits, and product quality checks. We do this to maintain high standards and secure buyer trust.

Stackly uses an advanced escrow-style payment framework. When you make a purchase, the money is secured until the vendor confirms shipment and tracking data is validated. We support major cards, PayPal, and multi-currency payouts.

Since Stackly is a multi-vendor platform, items in your cart might belong to different vendors. If so, they will ship separately directly from each vendor's warehouse. You will receive unique tracking numbers for each shipment.
