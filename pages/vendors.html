Vendor Program FAQs

Have questions about registration, commission structures, or shipping standards?

To sell on Stackly, you must provide a valid business registration license, corporate tax ID details, warehouse address verification, and catalog samples for quality evaluation. Onboarding typically takes 3-5 business days.

Stackly charges a flat 5% platform commission on completed sales transactions. There are no registration costs, listing fees, or monthly subscription fees for standard vendor profiles.

Seller payouts are disbursed weekly directly to your linked bank account, Payoneer, or PayPal. Funds are cleared 7 days after the buyer receives the package, matching our customer satisfaction escrow system timelines.
